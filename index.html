HIV Prevention: PrEP and PEP

HIV 预防：暴露前预防 (PrEP) 和暴露后预防 (PEP)

PrEP (Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis)

Purpose: PrEP is used before potential exposure to HIV. It is a preventive measure.

When to Use: It is taken on an ongoing basis by individuals at high risk of HIV infection.

Duration: PrEP involves taking a daily pill (e.g., Truvada or Descovy) consistently.

Who Might Use It: People at high risk of HIV, such as those with an HIV-positive partner, those with multiple sexual partners, people who inject drugs, or those who frequently engage in unprotected sex.

Effectiveness: When taken consistently, PrEP is highly effective at preventing HIV infection. It significantly reduces the risk of getting HIV from sex or injection drug use.

暴露前预防 (PrEP)

目的：PrEP 在可能暴露于 HIV 之前使用，是一种预防措施。

使用时间：高风险 HIV 感染者需持续服用。

持续时间：PrEP 涉及每天服用一种药片（如 Truvada 或 Descovy）。

使用人群：高风险 HIV 感染者，如有 HIV 阳性伴侣、多性伴侣、注射毒品者或频繁无保护性行为者。

有效性：如果持续服用，PrEP 在预防 HIV 感染方面非常有效。它显著降低了通过性行为或注射毒品感染 HIV 的风险。

PEP (Post-Exposure Prophylaxis)

Purpose: PEP is used after potential exposure to HIV. It is an emergency measure.

When to Use: It should be started as soon as possible after a suspected exposure to HIV, ideally within 72 hours.

Duration: PEP involves taking antiretroviral medications for 28 days.

Who Might Use It: People who think they have been exposed to HIV through unprotected sex, needle sharing, or occupational exposure (e.g., healthcare workers).

Effectiveness: When taken correctly, PEP is highly effective at preventing HIV infection. However, it is not 100% guaranteed.

暴露后预防 (PEP)

目的：PEP 在可能暴露于 HIV 后使用，是一种紧急措施。

使用时间：应尽快在怀疑暴露于 HIV 后开始使用，最好在 72 小时内。

持续时间：PEP 涉及服用抗逆转录病毒药物 28 天。

使用人群：认为自己通过无保护性行为、共用针头或职业暴露（如医护人员）暴露于 HIV 的人。

有效性：如果正确服用，PEP 在预防 HIV 感染方面非常有效，但不是 100% 保证。

Key Differences

Timing: PEP is for after potential exposure; PrEP is for before potential exposure.

Usage: PEP is a short-term treatment; PrEP is a long-term prevention strategy.

Target Groups: PEP is used by anyone potentially exposed to HIV; PrEP is used by individuals at ongoing high risk.

Both PEP and PrEP require medical supervision and prescription. They are part of comprehensive HIV prevention strategies, which also include regular testing, safe sex practices, and harm reduction for people who use drugs.

主要区别

时间点：PEP 用于可能暴露后；PrEP 用于可能暴露前。

使用方式：PEP 是短期治疗；PrEP 是长期预防策略。

目标群体：PEP 适用于任何可能暴露于 HIV 的人；PrEP 适用于持续高风险的个体。

PEP 和 PrEP 都需要医疗监督和处方。它们是全面 HIV 预防策略的一部分，还包括定期检测、安全性行为和毒品使用者的风险减少措施。

Screening Tests Before PrEP and PEP

Before starting PrEP or PEP, the following screenings are recommended:

  • HIV Test
  • Kidney Function Test
  • Hepatitis B Test
  • STI Screening
  • Pregnancy Test (if applicable)
  • Liver Function Test (for PEP)

PrEP 和 PEP 之前的筛查测试

在开始 PrEP 或 PEP 之前，建议进行以下筛查：

  • HIV 测试
  • 肾功能测试
  • 乙型肝炎测试
  • 性传播感染筛查
  • 怀孕测试（如适用）
  • 肝功能测试（用于 PEP）

Follow-Up and Monitoring

Regular follow-up is essential:

  • PrEP: Every three months for HIV testing, kidney function tests, and STI screenings.
  • PEP: HIV testing at the end of the 28-day course and several weeks after completing PEP.

后续跟进和监测

定期跟进非常重要：

  • PrEP：每三个月进行一次 HIV 检测、肾功能测试和性传播感染筛查。
  • PEP：在 28 天疗程结束时和完成 PEP 后几周进行 HIV 检测。
